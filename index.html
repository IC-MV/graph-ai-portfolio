About Me

Iconic blue‑cream Birman cat born on 15 August 2011, Choupette Lagerfeld rose from cherished companion of the late Chanel creative director Karl Lagerfeld to a multimillion‑earning feline fashion icon.

Today, she parlayed her pedigree and photogenic poise into high‑profile brand ambassadorships and a social‑media following that tops 260 000 fans on Instagram.

Key Career Highlights

  • Earned €3 million in 2014 across two major campaigns (Opel Corsa & Shu Uemura “Shupette”).
  • Star of the Opel Corsa 2015 global calendar photographed by Karl Lagerfeld.
  • Fronted Shu Uemura × Karl Lagerfeld holiday capsule “Shupette.”
  • Design muse for LucyBalu premium cat furniture line.
  • Featured in Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Vanity Fair, and People.
  • Campaign face of Hublot Big Bang 20ᵗʰ Anniversary (2025).
